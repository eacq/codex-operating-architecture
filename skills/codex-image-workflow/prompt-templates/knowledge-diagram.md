# Knowledge Diagram Prompt

Create a clean explanatory image for {{topic}}.

Purpose: {{purpose}}
Audience: {{audience}}
Aspect ratio: {{aspect_ratio}}

Visual requirements:

- Show the main entities, their relationships, and the direction of flow.
- Use a restrained professional palette with strong contrast.
- Keep labels short and readable; avoid dense paragraphs inside the image.
- Leave enough margin for embedding in Markdown or Obsidian notes.
- Prefer diagrammatic clarity over decorative detail.

Do not include:

- Watermarks, fake UI chrome, illegible microtext, or random decorative icons.
- Private names, credentials, account identifiers, or real screenshots.
