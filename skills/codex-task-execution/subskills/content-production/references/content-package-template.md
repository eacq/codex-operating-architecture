# Content Package Template

Use the headings that apply; omit empty optional sections rather than adding
placeholders to delivered content.

```markdown
# Content Brief: <working title>

## Reader and delivery
- Reader:
- Channel and language:
- Purpose and desired action:
- Deliverable and publication boundary:

## Evidence and constraints
- Source pointers:
- Confirmed claims / protected facts:
- Open questions or assumptions:
- Voice, accessibility, and copyright constraints:

## Plan
- One-sentence value proposition:
- Outline or sequence:
- Visual/deck need and format rationale:
- Review owner and acceptance checks:

## Draft and review record
- Final artifact:
- Claim/support check:
- Reader-value and channel-fit check:
- Visual/provenance or Office QA:
- Revisions and stop condition:
```

Keep sources as pointers or short claim notes. Do not paste credentials, private
sessions, copyrighted full text, or unpublished personal data into the brief.
