# Academic Manuscript Revision Boundaries

Academic polishing improves how an evidence-backed claim is expressed. It does
not invent evidence, alter results, or convert an uncertain observation into a
stronger conclusion.

## Revision Order

Diagnose the section role and argument chain first, repair paragraph relations
next, then revise local wording. This prevents a smooth sentence-level rewrite
from preserving an unclear overall argument.

## Protected Boundary

Numbers, units, conditions, technical terms, citations, equations, references,
field markup, claim direction, uncertainty, and limitations require explicit
preservation. In a DOCX, citation and equation runs are structural content, not
ordinary replaceable text.

## Observable Style Diagnosis

Report vague actors, decorative claims, unsupported novelty, generic value
language, stacked abstractions, or mechanical connectors as textual symptoms.
Do not infer that a named person or system authored the passage from style
alone.
