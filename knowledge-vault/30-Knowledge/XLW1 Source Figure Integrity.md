# XLW1 Source Figure Integrity

Figures support the algorithm and experimental evidence chain. They must not be converted into illustrative AI graphics or altered in ways that affect evidentiary meaning.

## Evidence

- Historical review repeatedly required preservation of source-image borders, axes, annotations, and traces.
- The project figure contract forbids cropping, relabelling, retouching, data invention, and AI bitmap regeneration.
- CAM screenshots may support validation but must not displace the discrete-geometry algorithm as the paper's central contribution.

## Invalidation

- Only an explicit author request with replacement source evidence can change a final figure's data-bearing content.
