# Academic Text Style Module

`codex-text-style` owns expression-level academic prose diagnosis, minimal revision, and style profiles.

## Ownership

- Audience, genre, language, and evidence-posture style profiles.
- Diagnosis or minimal revision of academic prose.
- Meaning protection and explicit unresolved-risk flags.
- Chinese, English, and bilingual academic wording.
- Whole-to-local manuscript revision and DOCX-safe text handoff.

## Non-Ownership

- Citation or claim-support verification.
- Research, data, method, and conclusion decisions.
- Document creation, tracked changes, or rendered layout repair.
- Venue rules not supplied from a current primary source.
- Locked-template DOCX package mutation or rendered layout repair.
