# Literature Discovery Workflow

1. Define the research question and inclusion boundaries.
2. Search multiple scholarly sources with `literature-search` or `nature-academic-search`.
3. Deduplicate identifiers and score relevance.
4. Hand selected records to [[Paper Reading and Evidence Workflow]].
5. Store accepted references through [[Zotero Reference Workflow]].

Do not assume one chat-compatible provider replaces domain literature APIs; see [[Provider Capability Boundary]].
