# Paper Reading and Evidence Workflow

1. Acquire legal full text and preserve DOI or source identifiers.
2. Extract claims, methods, datasets, limitations, figures, and exact evidence locations.
3. Separate author claims from Codex interpretation.
4. Link evidence into [[Academic Writing and Citation Workflow]] and visual requirements into [[Scientific Figure Workflow]].
5. Store bibliographic identity through [[Zotero Reference Workflow]].
